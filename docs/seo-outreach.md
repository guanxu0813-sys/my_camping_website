# SEO outreach playbook

Use this only after the report and CSV return `200` in production:

- Report: `https://www.campgearcompare.com/guides/camping-gear-weight-price-report-2026.html`
- CSV: `https://www.campgearcompare.com/data/reports/camping-gear-weight-price-2026.csv`

The goal is to earn relevant citations. Do not buy links, automate comments, or
post the same promotional message across communities.

## 1. Data Is Plural

Data Is Plural accepts dataset suggestions by email and prefers bulk-downloadable
data with clear methodology.

- Criteria: https://www.data-is-plural.com/faq/criteria/
- Public submission address listed there: `jsvine@gmail.com`

Suggested subject:

> Dataset suggestion: weights and prices for 554 camping gear products

Suggested message:

> Hi Jeremy — I maintain CampGear Compare, a catalog built from official brand
> product pages. I published a cleaned CSV of 554 tents, tarps, sleeping bags,
> sleeping pads, and stoves with representative weight, reference price,
> currency, product URL, and original source URL.
>
> Download: https://www.campgearcompare.com/data/reports/camping-gear-weight-price-2026.csv
>
> Methodology and summary:
> https://www.campgearcompare.com/guides/camping-gear-weight-price-report-2026.html
>
> The report explains exclusions and known currency/variant limitations. I
> thought the category medians and lightweight-product counts might be useful
> for outdoor or consumer-data analysis.

Send once. Do not follow up repeatedly.

## 2. Google Dataset Search

The report includes `Dataset` JSON-LD and a `DataDownload` link to the CSV.
No manual submission is required beyond submitting the normal sitemap in
Google Search Console. Validate the production page with:

https://search.google.com/test/rich-results

## 3. Outdoor Blog Network

Submission page: https://www.outdoorblog.net/submit/

The directory currently requires an English outdoor site to be active for at
least three months and to meet its other listed requirements. CampGear Compare
is not old enough yet. Recheck eligibility after the three-month mark rather
than submitting early.

## 4. Editorial outreach

Contact a small number of writers who have recently published quantitative
articles about ultralight tents, sleeping pads, or backpacking stoves. Reference
the exact statistic relevant to their article; do not ask for a generic link.

Template:

> Subject: Source data for your [topic] article
>
> Hi [name] — your article on [specific topic] prompted me to check the same
> question across our official-spec catalog. In the cleaned sample, [specific
> statistic]. The methodology and downloadable rows are here: [report URL].
> If it is useful for a future update, you are welcome to cite or analyze it.
> No action needed otherwise.

Keep a simple outreach log with the page contacted, date, relevant statistic,
and response. Stop after one message unless the recipient replies.

## 5. Community use

Reddit and forums should be treated as communities, not link directories. Link
only when the report directly answers an existing question, disclose that you
made the site, and include the useful result in the comment so the link is
optional.
