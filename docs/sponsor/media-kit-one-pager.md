## What we are

**CampGear Compare** is an English-language camping gear **spec comparison** site. Shoppers research tents, tarps, and sleeping bags in side-by-side tables sourced from **official brand websites**—then click through to buy on the brand’s store.

We help brands get **discovered at the moment of comparison**, without altering objective product data.
