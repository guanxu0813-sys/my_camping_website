### 1. Purpose

Publisher operates **CampGear Compare**, a camping gear specification comparison website (the “**Site**”). Sponsor wishes to purchase labeled promotional placement on the Site. Publisher agrees to deliver the placements described in **Schedule A**, subject to this Agreement.

---

### 2. Term

2.1 **Initial term:** **____** month(s), beginning on **____________** (“**Campaign Start Date**”) and ending on **____________** (“**Campaign End Date**”), unless terminated earlier under Section 10.

2.2 **Renewal:** Automatically renews for successive one-month periods at the then-current list price unless either Party gives **14 days’ written notice** of non-renewal before the next period.

---

### 3. Sponsorship services

3.1 Publisher will provide the placement(s) listed in **Schedule A** during the Term.

3.2 All sponsored content will be **clearly labeled** (e.g. “Sponsored” or as agreed in Schedule A) in compliance with applicable advertising disclosure laws, including FTC Endorsement Guides (U.S.) where relevant.

3.3 **Editorial firewall:** Sponsorship affects **display order and labeling only**. Publisher will **not** alter objective specification fields (including but not limited to weight, dimensions, capacity, materials, waterproof ratings, or price data) in favor of Sponsor unless such data is corrected from an official source with documented notice to Sponsor.

3.4 Publisher may reject or remove placements that, in Publisher’s reasonable judgment, harm editorial integrity, mislead users, or violate law or third-party rights.

3.5 Campaign configuration will use Sponsor’s approved `productId`(s) from Publisher’s catalog. Technical expiry will match Campaign End Date unless extended in writing.

---

### 4. Brand assets & license grant

4.1 **Sponsor materials** may include logos, product names, product images, short marketing copy, and URLs provided by Sponsor (“**Sponsor Materials**”).

4.2 Sponsor represents that it owns or controls the Sponsor Materials and has authority to grant the license below.

4.3 **License grant:** Sponsor grants Publisher a **non-exclusive, worldwide, royalty-free** license to use, reproduce, display, and adapt Sponsor Materials **solely** to:

- (a) perform the placements in Schedule A on the Site and related email/screenshots for campaign reporting;
- (b) attribute images per Site policy (e.g. “© [Brand] · Image from official site”);
- (c) create reasonable thumbnails or crops required by the Site layout.

4.4 **Scope limits:** No license for off-site advertising, resale, sublicensing, or use in unrelated products without separate written consent.

4.5 **Quality:** Sponsor will deliver web-ready assets within **5 business days** of Effective Date (or dates in Schedule A). Minimum: one logo (SVG or PNG), one product hero image if not already in Publisher catalog, and destination URL(s).

4.6 **Revocation:** If Sponsor revokes the asset license mid-term without cause, standard cancellation terms (Section 10.2) apply. Publisher will cease use within **48 hours** of valid written revocation, except where retention is required by law.

4.7 **Pre-existing catalog content:** Where the Site already displays product data scraped or linked from Sponsor’s official website, this Agreement **adds** explicit campaign asset permission for the sponsored placement period and does not transfer ownership of underlying IP.

**Sponsor additionally represents that:**

- (c) Sponsor Materials do not infringe third-party IP or publicity rights;

**Publisher represents that:**

- (f) it will implement agreed placements in materially accordance with Schedule A;
- (g) it will maintain a public Sponsor Policy describing labeling and editorial rules.

### 7. Indemnification

7.1 **Sponsor** will defend and indemnify Publisher against third-party claims arising from (i) Sponsor Materials, (ii) Sponsor’s products or services, or (iii) Sponsor’s breach of Section 6, except to the extent caused by Publisher’s gross negligence.

7.2 **Publisher** will defend and indemnify Sponsor against third-party claims that the Site’s placement implementation (excluding Sponsor Materials) infringes IP, except to the extent caused by Sponsor Materials or Sponsor’s breach.

7.3 The indemnified Party must promptly notify, cooperate, and allow control of defense (standard conditions).

### 8. Disclaimer & limitation of liability

8.1 EXCEPT AS EXPRESSLY STATED, THE SITE AND PLACEMENTS ARE PROVIDED “**AS IS**.” PUBLISHER DISCLAIMS IMPLIED WARRANTIES OF MERCHANTABILITY, FITNESS FOR A PARTICULAR PURPOSE, AND NON-INFRINGEMENT.

8.2 **No performance guarantee:** Publisher does not guarantee specific traffic, clicks, conversions, or revenue. Any metrics shared are **good-faith estimates or historical aggregates** unless expressly guaranteed in Schedule A.

8.3 **Liability cap:** Each Party’s total liability under this Agreement is limited to **fees paid or payable by Sponsor in the three (3) months** preceding the claim, except for (i) indemnification obligations, (ii) breach of Section 4 license scope, or (iii) willful misconduct.

8.4 Neither Party is liable for indirect, incidental, special, or consequential damages.

---

### 9. Confidentiality

Non-public business terms, unreleased products, and campaign performance data marked confidential shall not be disclosed except to advisors under need-to-know or as required by law.

---

### 10. Termination

10.1 **Convenience:** Either Party may terminate for convenience with **14 days’ written notice** effective at the end of the current paid billing period.

10.2 **Cause:** Either Party may terminate immediately for material breach uncured **10 days** after written notice.

10.3 **Effect:** Upon termination, Publisher removes placements within **48 hours** (or end of paid period, if later). Sections 4 (survival of license limits), 6–9, and 11 survive.

10.4 **Refunds:** If Publisher fails to deliver Schedule A placements for a full paid month, Sponsor receives pro-rata refund for that month. No refund for Sponsor convenience termination mid-period.
