How to choose

  • Weight: the Pro is about 140 g lighter using the representative trail weights in the official catalog.
  • Capacity: both are listed as two-person shelters.
  • Fabric choice: the Pro listing offers SilNylon / SilPoly options; check the selected variant before buying.
  • Decision: the standard Lanshan 2 suits buyers who want a separate inner; the Pro suits buyers prioritizing the lower listed trail weight.

Method and limitations

This is a specification comparison, not a hands-on review. Weights and prices can vary by size, fabric, season and market. We use representative values from brand listings and link the source so you can verify the exact variant before buying.
