Quick takeaways

  • Weight gap is huge: Cloud Up 2 is roughly 3.8 kg lighter — the main reason ultralight shoppers skip Amenity Dome.
  • Price gap matches use case: Amenity Dome 2 costs about 3–4× more; you’re paying for Snow Peak build quality and car-camp livability, not grams saved.
  • Same capacity, different mission: Both are marketed as 2-person tents, but Amenity Dome prioritizes vestibule space and weather-ready comfort; Cloud Up targets fast pitch and pack weight.
