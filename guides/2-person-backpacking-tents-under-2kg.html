How to use this shortlist

  • Check the weight basis: trail, minimum and packaged weights are not always equivalent.
  • Check the variant: fabric and inner-tent choices can change total weight.
  • Check support requirements: trekking-pole shelters may exclude the poles from listed weight.
  • Use price as a reference: currencies, promotions and stock change over time.

Methodology

We filtered the CampGear Compare official-source catalog for two-person tents with a positive representative weight at or below 2.0 kg, then manually removed accessories and records whose model name conflicted with the capacity field. This is a spec shortlist, not a hands-on ranking.
