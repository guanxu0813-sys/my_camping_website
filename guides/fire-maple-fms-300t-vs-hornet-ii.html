Fire Maple FMS-300T vs Hornet II

The FMS-300T and Hornet II are closely related titanium canister stoves. The 300T minimizes weight and price; the Hornet II adds a redesigned support platform for a small weight increase.

How to choose

  • Weight: the catalog rounds the 300T to about 40 g and the Hornet II to about 50 g.
  • Pot support: Fire Maple describes the Hornet II as the more stability-focused design.
  • Price: the 300T has the lower official reference price in the current catalog.
  • Decision: choose the 300T for minimum grams and cost; choose the Hornet II when its wider, redesigned supports are worth the small penalty.

Method and limitations

This is a specification comparison, not a hands-on review. Weights and prices can vary by size, fabric, season and market. We use representative values from brand listings and link the source so you can verify the exact variant before buying.
