About

Independent camping gear comparisons built from official brand specs — not a store.

What CampGear Compare is

CampGear Compare (campgearcompare.com) helps backpackers and campers compare tents, tarps, sleeping bags, sleeping pads, stoves, backpacks, and camp furniture side by side. We focus on structured specs — weight, capacity, materials, and reference prices — so you can research faster than flipping between brand sites.

We pay special attention to budget and Chinese ultralight brands (for example Naturehike, 3F UL Gear, Flame’s Creed, Aegismax, OneTigris, Asta Gear, Fire Maple) alongside established labels such as Snow Peak, Big Agnes, and Sea to Summit — a niche where English-language comparison tables are still sparse.

Who runs this site

CampGear Compare is an independently operated project. We are not a retailer, manufacturer, or authorized dealer. We do not sell gear directly and we do not hold inventory.

Where the data comes from

Product names, specs, descriptions, and images are sourced from each brand’s official website (or official storefront) whenever possible. Each listing keeps a link back to the original product page. Specs can change; always confirm critical details on the brand site before you buy.
