The Cape™ Lite 4-Person Instant Cabin Tent is a spacious and easy-to-set-up shelter designed for family camping, weekend getaways, and outdoor adventures. Featuring a pre-attached pole system, this instant tent sets up in just 60 seconds while offering excellent ventilation, reliable weather protection, and a comfortable interior for small families or groups. Key Features: 60-Second Instant Setup: Pre-attached poles allow quick and hassle-free setup, so you can spend less time pitching and more time enjoying the outdoors. Portable & Family-Friendly: Lightweight and foldable design with a carry bag makes transport and storage simple for camping trips and road adventures.
