Comparison Notes

Dragonfly™ Bikepack OSMO™ is an ultralight, freestanding tent built for bikepacking, canoe trips, and any adventure where pack-down size matters. Now featuring an improved pole structure and a unique crossbar that expands interior space, this tent travels light while letting you truly stretch your wings. Its stronger, more stable pole set pairs with NEMO’s custom Elements™ hardware, including a forged hub design that angles the crossbar “wings” upward before tensioning. This Precurve™ system creates a near-horizontal roof and generous headroom across the tent, not just at the peak. Axial™ Lite corner anchors simplify rainfly attachment, making setup quick and effortless. Inside, Dragonfly Bikepack OSMO™ is as resilient as it is roomy. Its solution-dyed, PFAS-free proprietary fabric repels water, resists tearing, and stays taut even in heavy rain. Generous doors and spacious vestibules pair with a Landing Zone™ storage tub to keep gear protected from mud. A gear loft, multiple pockets, and the Nightlight Pocket™ provide organization and soft, even illumination from your headlamp. A configurable daisy chain inside or out helps dry wet gear or keep essentials in reach. When it’s time to move, the tent packs neatly into its bike-optimized dry bag, sized for easy packing and secure attachment to your headtube and handlebars—no wobble, no hassle.
