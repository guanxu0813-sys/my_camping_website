Comparison Notes

The Static V Summit™ is designed to keep you warm and comfortable, even in winter conditions. Its body-mapped shape and signature V-chamber design minimize air movement to maximize insulation and comfort. Each chamber is filled with lightweight, durable Klymalite™ synthetic insulation, adding crucial protection from the cold ground. Dynamic side rails keep you centered on your pad, so you sleep soundly through the night. Designed for backcountry travel, it’s ultralight, packs down small, and inflates quickly when you reach camp. As part of the Summit Series, the Static V Summit™ is crafted for dedicated adventurers who want elevated warmth, refined comfort, and reliable performance for demanding backcountry trips.
