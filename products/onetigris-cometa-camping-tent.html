Comparison Notes

Why Choose OneTigris COMETA Camping Tent? One of the gems of the Black Tigris Series, the COMETA is a carbon black baker style tent, offering a tunnel-like interior with a front porch setup for your camping furniture. COMETA refers to comet in Latin, and this tent is the perfect home base for star gazing in the wild. We used light and durable 40D nylon fabric with single-layered silicone coating for up to 3000mm waterproof rating, and designed it with easy setup in mind, meaning minimal tent poles and guy-line fixes. Campers can enter/exit from the double-layered front entrance or unzip the side entrance, and in the summer simply use the outer tent as a sun shade/temporary outdoor shelter. Ideal for 2 campers, you can also opt for a camp cot for one only using the outer tent.
