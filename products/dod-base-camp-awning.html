Extend your cozy living area by adding a covered porch to your Uchi or Tsuku tent. The Base Camp Awning is a designed to match the Uchi Tent or Tsuku Base Tent doors with a V shaped side that allows complete coverage where the awning and tent meet. The Base Camp Awning is a component of the Case Camp Bundle. Made from DOD's revolutionary Canvo ™ fabric. Poles included. Expands living space of the Uchi Tent & Tsuku Base Tent. Includes three poles. Crafted from durable Canvo ™ fabric. V-shaped design matches seamlessly to the Uchi or Tsuku. Also works as an independent tarp shelter.
