Comparison Notes

Our Seamless Down Hugger 800 #3 Long is a state-of-the-art sleeping bag. Its light weight (clocking in at a minimal 531g) doesn’t come at the expense of performance, as it has a comfort temperature rating of 4°C. It can even go as low as -1°C when bolstered correctly. How’ve we achieved this? Well-thought-out details and premium materials of course! Insulated by 800-fill power EX down, our most premium down, the exterior of the sleeping bag is made with our innovative baffle-less Spider System, specifically designed to not restrict loft. On the interior is our patented Super Spiral Stretch System, a feature that reliably retains heat by moving naturally with your body when you’re tossing-and-turning in the night and draws insulation closer to you. Finally, an insulated zip guard shuts out cold air, and is reinforced with triangulated gussets for extra durability.
