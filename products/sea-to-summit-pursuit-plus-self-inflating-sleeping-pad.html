Made for comfort-seeking outdoors lovers, The Pursuit Plus is a 7.5cm thick, self-inflating sleeping mat for maximum comfort in the wilderness. Available in the lightweight Contour or complete Rectangular versions, both use Horizontal Delta Coring construction to create foam-supported air channels for maximum comfort at a minimum weight. Pursuit Plus is built with bluesign APPROVED, RCS-recycled, solution-dyed fabric to create a mat with a high level of comfort and a low environmental impact.
