Comparison Notes

THE Dome Tent epitomizes classic tent design with its orthodox dome shape and crossed poles. Its simplicity makes for easy setup, and the added front room offers a relaxing space. This tent comes with a ground sheet to protect it and is made with durable 150D fabric. It features ample space, with a high ceiling in the inner tent and a large front room that can be extended into a canopy area for additional living space. (For a limited time, comes with Kiso Tarp Poles for awning). Classic dome design with easy-to-assemble cross-frame structure. Spacious interior suitable for up to 3 people. Large doors and windows ensure cool summers and warm springs/autumns. Includes a ground sheet for floor protection and easy setup. Made with durable 150D fabric for light blocking and longevity.
