Comparison Notes

Why Choose OneTigris STELLA Camping Tent? One of the gems of the Black Tigris Series, the dome-shaped STELLA is a low-key beauty when set up and testifies to its name which means “star” in Latin. While it looks similar to the SCAENA and COSMITTO backpacking tents, STELLA is a 4-season, 3000mm waterproof rated, carbon black camping shelter, with a mesh inner layer for good airflow, and a breathable solid outer layer for adequate heat retention. Designed with the most straightforward setup method via flexible poles that cross at the top of the dome and are securely anchored to the floor on all 4 corners, you have a quick and easy outdoor setup for use to protect you against the elements.
