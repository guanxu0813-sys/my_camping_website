Comparison Notes

Naturehike LD350 is a versatile four-season rectangular sleeping bag designed with an innovative top and bottom splicing system to maximize space utilization. Unlike conventional left and right splicing zippers that can be uncomfortable and reduce warmth, this design allows you to lie freely in any position. When feeling cold, tighten the drawstrings on both sides to transform the hood into a snug, warmer fit.
