Comparison Notes

The Ikos tents build on the design advances made in our award-winning ultralight Telos Tension Ridge tents. They feature the same great livability and versatile setup options for everyday backpacking and vehicle-supported camping in a larger, more comfort-oriented format. Four inner pockets and a gear loft provide plenty of space for your gear, and the rectangular tub floor offers room for two regular rectangular sleeping pads in the TR2 or room for three regular rectangular sleeping pads in the TR3. Sea to Summit material quality and attention to detail complete the package. The floor is a 68D polyester with a 4,000mm hydrostatic head, and a footprint which extends to the tips of the vestibules is available. A DAC MX poleset supports an 1800mm 68D polyester ripstop rainfly that is flame retardant-free and treated with a C0 non-PFC DWR. Machined-aluminum pole feet/rainfly C-clips make pitching easy. If your focus is comfort rather than gram counting, and you're looking for a tent for two or three people ( maybe a dog) that you can count on- the Ikos TR2 and TR3 could be your amazingly spacious home away from home. Features Our unique Tension Ridge maximizes the livable space versus the footprint of our tents, offering a lot more room around the head and shoulders. Tension Ridge allows us to place a wide vent at the highest point of the tent, helping minimize condensation build-up.
