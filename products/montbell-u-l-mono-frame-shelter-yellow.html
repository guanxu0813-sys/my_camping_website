Comparison Notes

The U.L. Mono Frame Shelter is far-and-away our lightest and most compact tent. In fact, it might just be the lightest and most compact the market has to offer. That’s a bold claim, but we’re confident in it. It’s all thanks to the 10-denier Ballistic Airlight nylon ripstop (a fabric we frequently use for our lightest jackets) and the single-wall, single pole construction. The angle of said pole is aligned at a 35 degree angle which vastly increases the surface area of liveable space. Weather protection isn’t an issue either, as the sealed seams effectively prevent water ingress. And for the moisture that builds up on the inside of the tent, there are three strategically-placed ventilation panels for excellent airflow.

At about 0.61 kg, the Montbell U.L. Mono Frame Shelter (Yellow) is lighter than 93% of the 301 tents in our database (category median 2.38 kg). Its reference price of 430 GBP is above the category median of 227.46 GBP.
