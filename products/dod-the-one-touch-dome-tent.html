Comparison Notes

THE 1Pull Dome Tent combines ease of setup with spacious comfort, accommodating up to 3 people. Its 1Pull mechanism allows for hassle-free assembly. DOD’s muted color palette adds a touch of elegance to your camping experience. The tent includes a front chamber for additional space, making it perfect for both relaxation and storage. Large footprint awning extends living area and provides shade. (For a limited time, comes with Kiso Tarp Poles for awning). Effortless 1Pull setup for quick and convenient assembly. Spacious interior with a high ceiling and large front room comfortably fits 3 people. Large doors and windows provide cool ventilation in summer. Ground sheet included for added floor protection and durability.
