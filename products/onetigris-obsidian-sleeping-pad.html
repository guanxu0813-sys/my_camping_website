W hy Choose OneTigris OBSIDIAN Sleeping Pads? The OSIDIAN is a superior sleeping pad because of its high thermal resistance property that offers excellent insulation to prevent the loss of body heat during use. It weighs only 2.05lb (0.93kg) for easy carry, measuring 3.1 inches (8cm) in thickness for extra comfort. There are a variety of sleeping pads on the market: Foam pads that are cheap, light, but take up space and aren’t as comfortable; self-inflating mats that expand on their own when spread out but are also bulky and less comfy; high-end options that are sturdy, well insulated, lightweight, but cost hundreds of dollars and aren’t cheap or easy to repair. We know there is a need for a highly functional but more reasonably priced sleep mat option. That’s where the OBSIDIAN comes in. Using lightweight 20D nylon fabric as its outer shell, with 3 sheets of TPU membranes and 3 layers of aluminum coating, it’s designed to maximize insulation during use so you can stay cozy all night long. The product comes with an air pumping device that is quick and easy to use, and a pair of tear-fixing patches so you can stay prepared when on the road. It’s 6.2ft by 2ft to fit most users, offering a quality lie-down anywhere you go.
