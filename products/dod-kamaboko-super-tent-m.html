Comparison Notes

DOD Outdoors’ multi-functional super tent, the Kamaboko, is the centerpiece of the perfect base camp. Comfort in camp requires more than just a place to sleep. You also need a place to get out of the sun, out of the rain, away from the bugs, or just get some privacy. The Kamaboko offers all that. A range of accessories sold separately let you customize the tent to exactly meet your needs. The Kamaboko comes with a sleeping chamber that attaches to the ribs and can easily be removed when not in use. Super Tent – pitch smaller tents inside, or use as a bug tent, sun/rain tarp. Comes with one hanging sleeping chamber that sleeps 5 adults. An additional optional sleeping chamber (sold separately) can be hung on opposite side to double sleeping capacity to 10 adults. High ceilings and wide span provide lots of unobstructed space. Goes up fast, packs up small and light. End doors double as awnings; awning poles not included. No floor so you can walk in and out without removing shoes or tracking in dirt. Front, back, and side panels zip up to expose mesh panels for increased airflow. Colors: Tan Capacity: Fits 5 adults per inner tent (one included). Total capacity with 2 inner tents is 10 adults. Dimensions/footprint: Please refer to picture diagram for full dimensions. This is a multi-room, tunnel, basecamp tent. Area: 210sqft Length and Width: 21 in x 10 in (53.34 cm x 25.4 cm) Inner Height: 5.11 ft (180 cm) Packed size: L x W x H: 27 in x 13.8 in x 12.2 in (69 cm x 35 cm x 31 cm)
