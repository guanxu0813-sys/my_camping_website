Comparison Notes

For hunts where weight and speed are the top concern, a simplified shelter is a must. The Scalar™ multi-configuration camp tarp enhances the protection of a classic tarp and includes time-saving details that ensure a quick setup and pack down — because when game is on the move, there’s no time to lose. Designed in collaboration with the experts at First Lite — and featuring their proprietary Cipher™ camouflage pattern — Scalar has integrated G-hooks and webbing loops so it can easily close at one end for 3-wall protection from the elements. Built-in stash pockets at each corner store the necessary guyline for tensioning, and additional pole pockets allow trekking poles to easily stand in as tent poles, saving even more weight. (Trekking poles not included.) For versatile, lightweight protection, keep Scalar stashed in your pack for unexpected conditions and backup.

Its reference price of 124.95 USD is below the category median of 214.5 USD.
