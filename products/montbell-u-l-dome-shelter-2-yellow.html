Comparison Notes

Our U.L. Dome Shelter 2 is one of the lightest and most compact tents on the market. A bold claim of course, but we’re confident in our claim. It’s all thanks to the 10-denier Ballistic Airlight nylon ripstop (a fabric we frequently use for our lightest jackets) and a freestanding, single-wall, domed construction. Despite the tent being so thin, it has excellent weather-protection, thanks to the sealed seams that effectively prevent water ingress. The upper part of the entrance is a double-layered structure that allows for ventilation that blocks out water as well. And if all that wasn’t enough, it’s also incredibly easy to pitch thanks to the sleeved poles.
