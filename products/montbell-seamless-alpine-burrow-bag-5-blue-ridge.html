Comparison Notes

Our Seamless Alpine Burrow Bag #5 is a summer sleeping bag designed to excel in humid conditions. This is because it utilises synthetic insulation which, unlike down, stays warm even when it’s wet. Using our specialised EXCELOFT insulation, the bag is temperature rated to 8°C. The double-layer construction is reliably warm, and features our patented Super Spiral Stretch system on the interior. This retains heat by moving naturally with your body when you’re tossing-and-turning in the night and draws insulation closer to you. Which is exactly where you want it to be!
