Brave the extreme cold with the XR1500 Snowmelt Down Sleeping Bag , designed for professional-grade warmth and comfort in temperatures as low as -35°C . This high-performance sleeping bag combines advanced insulation technology with durable, water-resistant materials to keep you warm, dry, and comfortable in the harshest winter conditions. Whether you're camping in snowy mountains or exploring icy terrains, the XR1500 ensures superior heat retention and ultimate coziness. Key Features: Ultra-Warmth Design : Features a 90% duck down filling with 650FP and hollow cotton insulation for exceptional heat retention, even in extreme cold. Water-Resistant Fabric : The 30D nylon outer shell and 70D nylon bottom are treated with a water-repellent coating to protect against moisture and dampness. Comfortable & Breathable : The 300T polyester lining ensures softness and breathability, while the 3D foot box provides extra space and warmth for your feet. Dual-Layer Insulation : Unique combination filling technology prevents heat loss from the bottom, with hollow cotton (160GSM x 3) offering additional warmth, moisture resistance, and breathability.
