Comparison Notes

TerraGrid™ IGT Modular Camping Table combines a charcoal grill and camping table in one compact, travel friendly design, making it ideal for outdoor cooking, camping, picnics, and on the road adventures. Featuring a single IGT module system, it can be expanded with other Naturehike IGT units to create a customized outdoor cooking setup. Quick to assemble, lightweight, and foldable, it is designed for easy transport and everyday outdoor use. Key Features 2 in 1 BBQ Grill and Table - Functions as both a charcoal grill and a stable camping table, offering flexible use for cooking and dining outdoors. Expandable IGT System - Built with one IGT module and compatible with Naturehike folding grills, stoves, and extension units, allowing you to build your own outdoor kitchen layout. Quick Setup Design - Tool free assembly lets you set up in seconds so you can start grilling or relaxing right away. Foldable and Portable - Lightweight and foldable design makes it easy to carry, store, and transport for camping or travel. Thoughtful Details - Includes a detachable lantern stand, and the grill lid can also be used as a tabletop extension for added workspace. Easy to Clean - Removable charcoal brazier with enamel coating helps resist rust and grease, making cleanup after cooking quick and simple.
