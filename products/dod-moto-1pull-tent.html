Comparison Notes

The Moto 1Pull Tent is a small footprint two-room tent. The design is perfect for campers who appreciate the advantages of separate rooms in the same tent, but still need a smaller tent. It's inspired by motorcycle touring tents, but works great for any type of camping. DOD's 1Pull system allows quick setup and take down. The inner chamber is mesh-enclosed for sleeping. The outer chamber, or vestibule, is a place to hang out away from sun, rain, or bugs. In the evening, it's a safe space to park a bicycle, motorcycle, or gear. Two-room structure provides distinct spaces. Compact and lightweight, making it easy to transport on any adventure. Large vestibule area, perfect for storing equipment or enjoying the view. DOD's one-pull system allows for quick setup and take down. Durable construction ensuring reliable shelter in various weather conditions.
