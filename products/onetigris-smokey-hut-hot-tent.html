Why Choose OneTigris SMOKEY HUT Camping Tent? Based off of our classic teepee-shaped tents that are lightweight, easy to set up, and great to look at, the SMOKEY HUT is a 4-season-tent that can be used also for winter camping, with a flue pipe opening for burning stoves and snow skirts for optimal heat retention. The coyote brown color option uses 210T polyester taffeta that is durable, wrinkle-resistant, and easy to care for. The beige color option uses 20D nylon fabric which is slightly lighter, equally durable, and highly resistant to dirt/water/abrasion. Both color options offer 2000mm waterproofing, quality YKK® snag-free zippers, heat-taped seams, and small vents at the top of the tent for good airflow. Note that this tent doesn’t include an inner tent but does come with a floor mat, stove jack, guy-lines, stakes, and a tent pole. It sleeps up to 2 campers (the floor mat is sized for 1 camper) and weighs only around 4.5lb (2kg), making it a highly ideal, lightweight option for using on the road.   • This product does NOT have a tent bottom but it comes with a floor mat that fits 1 camper.
