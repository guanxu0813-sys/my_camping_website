Comparison Notes

Blacktail tents are a tried and true Big Agnes classic - light enough for the backcountry, and strong enough for the front country. The Blacktail Hotel is ideal for those who need some extra room, without the weight and bulk of a car camping specific tent. Designed with a generous oversized vestibule on one side of the tent - perfect for storing large packs, sheltering four-legged friends, or simply for hanging out in on rainy days. Burly recycled fabric means that you won't have to sacrifice durability for weight savings or sustainability - you get it all! Spacious sleeping areas and easy access through two side-entry doors make early morning entry and exit with a tentmate a breeze when nature calls. No matter where your adventure takes you, camp comfy in a Blacktail!

At about 3.09 kg, the Big Agnes Blacktail Hotel 3 sits on the heavier side of our 301-model tents database (median 2.38 kg). Its reference price of 399.95 USD is above the category median of 227.46 USD.
