W hy Choose OneTigris JADE Sleeping Pads? The JADE is an inflatable air pad made for spring, summer, and fall, offering a 3D structured insulation system that’s extra firm when the pad’s been inflated to offer superior support for the sleeper. Made extra wide for a better shut eye, it measures 6.5ft by 2.3ft to comfortably sleep most sleepers. To set it up, an air pump is included with the product, and it only takes 4-6 times to fully inflate. While its sister product, the OBSIDIAN, is lighter and warmer, the JADE uses 40D nylon and extra filling for more durability and sleep support, and is an excellent option for non-winter outdoor use. There’s also a set of tear-fixing patches so you can stay prepared on the road, and a handy stuff sack for easy carry and transportation.
