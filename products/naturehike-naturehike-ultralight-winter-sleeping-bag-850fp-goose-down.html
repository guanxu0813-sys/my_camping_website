Naturehike Winter Sleeping Bag 850FP Goose Down - Ultralight & All-Season Comfort Experience unparalleled warmth and comfort with Naturehike's J-series gaiter bags, designed to endure harsh winter conditions down to -30 degrees Celsius. Available in models JA700, JA1000, and JA1300, these sleeping bags feature premium 850FP goose down fill, ensuring optimal insulation and lightweight performance. The fill weights vary across the models: 7009 for JA700, 1000 for JA1000, and 1300 for JA1300, providing tailored warmth for every adventurer. Ethical Down Standard (RDS) Certified Our commitment to ethical practices is reinforced by our adherence to the Responsible Down Standard (RDS). This international certification guarantees that our down comes from ducks and geese not subjected to live island harvesting or force-feeding. Each batch undergoes traceability audits to ensure humane treatment throughout the supply chain. Premium Construction for Ultimate Durability Crafted with a robust 5D nylon outer layer, these sleeping bags are waterproof and resistant to wear and tear. The inner liner and muffler are made of high-quality 5D bonjie fabric, offering excellent moisture absorption, breathability, and smoothness. The sponge fabric adds extra warmth, lightness, and durability while maintaining quick-drying properties and superior elasticity. Innovative Insulation Design Featuring a layered sealed structure, this design prevents feather displacement, ensuring consistent warmth. Accura
