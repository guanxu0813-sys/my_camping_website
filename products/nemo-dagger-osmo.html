Comparison Notes

The most livable backpacking tent in our line just got a little roomier. Dagger OSMO now features an updated pole structure that adds 4 inches to the door height, 2 inches to the peak height, and 22% more usable space to the vestibule. This maximized interior offers downright luxurious downtime in camp and comes at no extra weight cost. Rounding out the improved experience is NEMO’s custom Elements™ hardware suite, featuring the Axial™ corner anchor for one-handed setup and tensioning. The updated hardware and poles are paired with solution-dyed OSMO™ fabrics on the fly and floor that promise 4x longer lasting water repellency, 3x less stretch when wet, and a 20% higher strength rating than similar fabrics. Made from 100% recycled yarns for reduced waste, this OSMO fabric also requires less water for the dyeing process. These sustainability efforts combine to earn Dagger OSMO certification as a bluesign® product backpacking tent — the industry’s first at the time of launch. Features like the Divvy Cube™ stuff sack, Landing Zone™ waterproof storage tub, Nightlight Pockets™, and one-handed Gatekeeper™ door clips remain staples of this backpacking favorite, equipping it well for both relaxed car camping and backcountry treks. Between the feature set, ample interior, and light weight, Dagger OSMO is a true one-tent wonder for adventures of all kinds.
