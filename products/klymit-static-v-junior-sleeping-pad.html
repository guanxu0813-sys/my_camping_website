Comparison Notes

Our most popular pad trimmed to a ¾ length that’s perfect for ounce counters, kids and Scouts, the Static V Short™ packs to the size of a soda can, and weighs a scant 14 ounces. The body-mapped V chamber design limits air movement to help maintain insulating performance and ergonomic comfort, dynamic side rails keep you centered on the pad, and the durable material is tough enough to handle direct contact with the ground when you want to travel light and leave the tent at home.
