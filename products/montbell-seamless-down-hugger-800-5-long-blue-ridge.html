Comparison Notes

Our Seamless Down Hugger 800 #5 Long is a state-of-the-art, two season sleeping bag. Lightweight and rated to a comfort temperature of 8°C, it’s insulated by 800 fill power EX Down, our most premium down. The exterior of the sleeping bag is made with our innovative baffle-less Spider System, specifically designed to not restrict loft. On the interior is our patented Super Spiral Stretch system, a feature that reliably retains heat by moving naturally with your body when you’re tossing-and-turning in the night and draws insulation closer to you. Finally, an insulated zip guard shuts out cold air, and is reinforced with triangulated gussets for extra durability.
