Comparison Notes

Why Choose OneTigris Mesh Inner Tent 04? Designed mainly for use with our ROCK FORTRESS Hot Tent, this mesh shelter offers protection against ants, mosquitoes, snakes and other creatures that might disturb your sleep, with quality polyester fabric that’s strong and wrinkle/tear resistant, and a 4000mm waterproofed tent bathtub floor with taped seams for keeping campers and gear dry. The mesh tent is shaped like a half-dome with dual entrances featuring snag-free two-way-zippers for more convenient entry/exit. A setup kit of pegs, guy-lines, outer tent attachment carabiners are included with this product. Note that the ROCK FORTRESS fits up to 6 campers without the inner tent, while the compatible mesh inner tent is half the size to fit up to 3 campers. OneTigris Mesh Inner Tent 04 Polyester fabric construction, also known as terylene or dacron fabric, that is highly durably and wrinkle/tear resistant 4000mm waterproof rated bathtub floor with waterproof taped seams to keep you and your gear dry, and a camp light ceiling attachment point within Half-dome shape to fit up to 3 campers with a two-way-zippered entrance on each side for convenient entry/exit; the entrances can be rolled up and stowed out of the way Comes with tent pegs, 1 guy-line, and 2 carabiners for easy attachment to compatible outer tents such as the OneTigris ROCK FORTRESS Hot Tent Prevents insects such as ants, flies and mosquitoes, and wild animals such as snakes Note 1.
