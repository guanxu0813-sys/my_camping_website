The Kama Tarp creates a massive covered area to your campsite or backyard. The tarp is made to pair with the Kamaboko Tent but works equally well as a stand alone tarp. The Kama is made of durable Canvo ™ fabric which which is stronger than polyester and casts deep, cool shadows, great for summer camping. Canvo, unlike polyester, won't melt if it catches a campfire spark. The square shape with many attachment points allow for versatile setups. (For a limited time, comes with pegs, set of two standard poles, and one extra tall pole to lift the center of the tarp). Canvo ™ fabric for deep shade and longer life.
