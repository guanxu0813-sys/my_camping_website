The Naturehike Snowbird SP400 is a 2025 upgraded ultralight down sleeping bag engineered for cold-weather camping and backpacking. Filled with 400g of premium RDS & IDS certified duck down (650 fill power), it balances warmth and weight for reliable performance in temperatures as low as 38.8℉. The SP400 features a wind-blocking draft tube, C-shaped wrap design, and internal storage pocket, all designed to retain body heat while maintaining a compact carry size. Ideal for hikers, campers, and alpine adventurers seeking serious warmth without extra bulk.
