Comparison Notes

Our Big Tarp HX is an excellent lightweight shelter, ideal for gram-conscious thru-hikers and long-distance bikepackers. It’s made from a flame-resistant, 75-denier polyester taffeta that’s been coated with waterproof polyurethane, with up to 1,500 millimetres of water protection. There are six lashing holes, allowing you to pitch the tarp as you please and is compatible with our Aluminium Tarp Poles. Reflective guylines, aluminium pegs and a stuff sack all come included.
