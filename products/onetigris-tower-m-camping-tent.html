Comparison Notes

Why Choose OneTigris TOWER M Camping Tent? Inspired by the drum towers in old Asian cities, the TOWER M is an extra large camping shelter that fits a family and some more. The tent height is 6.2ft (190cm) and the interior space measures 13.1 sq. ft. Campers can comfortably fit multiple camping cots or sleeping bags, some camping furniture, and still have space left for storing luggage and gear. Using 40D nylon that is durable and highly dirt/water/abrasion resistant, with a layer of PU coating for 3000mm waterproofing and UPF50+ sun protection, it’s great for keeping campers protected from the elements. The TOWER M opens on 4 sides with domed entrances, offering a great view and good airflow. Thanks to its special dome-shaped design and 8-piece aluminum tent frame, the TOWER M is highly wind-resistant and gorgeous to look at. Equipped with a stove pipe opening for winter camping, use of premium fabric, and excellent heat retention, campers can camp out in the snow, staying cozy and warm without worrying about snow buildup. In short, the TOWER M is a great option for outdoor lovers everywhere for all seasons. Highlights

  • This tent does not have a tent floor and TPU/mesh doors; please purchase your own floor mat.
  • Set up on even ground and remove twigs and other sharp objects to prevent damage to the fabric.
  • For longer shelf life, air out to dry after use in the rain and store the product in a cool dry space.
  • Never leave your tent while the wood stove is in use. Always use CO detectors and maintain good ventilation while burning wood stoves inside your tent.
