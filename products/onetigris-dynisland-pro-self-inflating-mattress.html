Innovative Y-shape Cutting andstretching process Built-in air pump, no additional configuration required High R value, effectively insulates against ground cold Notes on R-values Why Choose DYNISLAND PRO SELF-INFLATING MATTRESS? The DYNISLAND PRO, an upgraded version of the DYNISLAND Double, is a springy, exceptionally comfortable outdoor mattress. With a thickness of 4 inches, it features a built-in pump for automatic inflation, eliminating the need for a separate pump. This all-season sleeping pad comes with a pump bag that inflates in just 2-3 strokes. Utilizing the latest fabric cutting and stretching technology, the pad inflates and deflates quickly. Once deflated, campers will find a specially designed crease in the center for easy folding and rolling, saving space. Sturdy side trim, similar to bedroom mattresses, provides added stability and enhances sleep quality. When folded, the DYNISLAND is smaller than most double sleeping pads on the market, yet it can support up to 440 lbs (200 kg), making it an ideal inflatable bed for solo campers, couples, and families. It's made of breathable, water-resistant 30D polyester that's soft, durable, and tear-resistant, while the bottom fabric is made of durable 190T fabric with non-slip grip points for a sound sleep.
