Comparison Notes

Add insect protection to your ultralight shelter system — the Escapist Mesh Bug Shelter features high-visibility 15D no-see-um mesh and a zippered door to block mosquitoes, ants and spiders. Hang it easily under the Escapist or Minimalist tarp, or pitch it using trekking poles or suspend it from trees. The waterproof Ultra-Sil LS tub floor wraps up the sides for reliable ground protection. Ultralight, compact and fast-drying, it’s the ideal bug-proof companion for tarp camping. Features Maximize insect protection - ultralight 15D no-see-um mesh with zippered entry provides complete defense against mosquitoes, ants and spiders Flexible setup options - suspends easily under tarps or pitches independently with trekking poles, guy lines, stakes or natural anchors Full ground protection - durable, waterproof, low-stretch Ultra-Sil LS 15D nylon/30D polyester tub floor with extended height for privacy and weather defense Spacious interior - floor plan fits two regular tapered sleeping mats with enough headspace to sit up comfortably inside the shelter Rapid tensioning - adjustable line tensioners accessible from under the tarp enable precise setup control and optimal fabric tension for a tight pitch Lower impact materials - Ultra-Sil LS fabric is recycled, bluesign APPROVED and non-PFAS.
