Comparison Notes

Add insect protection to your ultralight shelter system — the Escapist Mesh Bug Shelter features high-visibility 15D no-see-um mesh and a zippered door to block mosquitoes, ants and spiders. Hang it easily under the Escapist or Minimalist tarp, or pitch it using trekking poles or suspend it from trees. The waterproof Ultra-Sil LS tub floor wraps up the sides for reliable ground protection. Ultralight, compact and fast-drying, it’s the ideal bug-proof companion for tarp camping.
