Comparison Notes

The Penta Tarp is a minimalist shelter for small groups. Use the Penta Tarp as an addition to floating trips or for camping on a river. Intended for use near the water, the Penta Tarp features a unique design that allows a paddle to be used as a pole. The Penta Tarp is durable, water-resistant, and compact - an easy addition to any excursion. Please note: Light Tarp Poles are not included. Pair the Penta Tarp with the Light Tarp Pole 150cm for set-up.
