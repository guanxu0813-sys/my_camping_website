Comparison Notes

The Japanese-designed Land Lock is a tent and shelter combination for up to four adults. Use it for family or friend group camping trips and never worry about space. The Land Lock features a spacious gathering area, a private inner tent for sleeping, and two expandable shade panels for additional living space. The Land Lock also features sealable mesh windows that allow for cross breezes during hot summer nights. Pair the Land Lock with the Landlock Inner Mat for a comfortable sleeping set-up.
