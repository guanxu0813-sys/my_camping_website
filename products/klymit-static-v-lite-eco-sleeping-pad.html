Comparison Notes

The Static V Lite Eco™ is a lightweight three-season backpacking sleeping pad. The Static V Lite Eco™ features Klymit’s signature Body Mapping Technology and patented V-Chamber design. Side rails are integrated into the patterning and construction of the sleeping pad to keep you securely centered on the pad while inhibiting air movement while tossing and turning during the night. The Static V Lite Eco is the perfect pad for lightweight adventures.
