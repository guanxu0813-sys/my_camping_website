Comparison Notes

Unlock a new level of comfort around the campsite with the Camp Quillow. With the versatile Camp Quillow, easily convert between a quilt for staying cozy by the fire, and a pillow for quality backcountry rest. For maximum warmth in quilt mode use the corner hand pockets to wrap it around your shoulders. For hands-free use, fasten the clip to wear the quilt as a cape, keeping you warm while you're up and about. When you're ready for bed stuff it into the integrated pocket to use it as a comfy camp pillow!
