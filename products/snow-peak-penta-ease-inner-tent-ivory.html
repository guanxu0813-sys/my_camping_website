Comparison Notes

The Penta Ease 1 in Ivory is an inner tent designed to pair with the Penta Tarp in Ivory and can also be used alone. The tent features a front panel divided by a vertical zipper, interior loops, and interior pockets. The front panel consists of water-resistant fabric layered with breathable mesh and rain cover to prevent rain from entering. Pair the Penta Ease 1 in Ivory with the Penta Tarp in Ivory and Light Tarp Pole for complete protection from weather elements. Note: the Light Tarp Pole 150cm is sold separately.
