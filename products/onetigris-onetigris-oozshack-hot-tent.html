Why Choose OneTigris Onetigris CozShack Hot Tent? Shack means a cabin, CozShack means a cozy cabin, especially in winter days. The CozShack is a tent-like structure that completely shields a hammock or hot tent camper from the wind and rain, enabling the use of tent stoves with a built-in stove jack .and the CozShack is the home away from home that can be set up between trees or set up on flat ground via poles through its unique build and multiple tie-out points. It’s a compact and cost-efficient camping solution for bushcrafters and outdoor loving folks to adventure within all kinds of terrains.
