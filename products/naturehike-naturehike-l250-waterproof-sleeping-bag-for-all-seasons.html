Comparison Notes

Naturehike L250 is a versatile rectangular sleeping bag designed for year-round outdoor use. Featuring a unique DIY top and bottom splicing system, it enhances space utilization and comfort. Unlike common side zippers that can be uncomfortable and reduce warmth, this design lets you move freely and adjust the hood’s drawstring for a snug fit and extra warmth when needed. Key Features: All-Season Warmth – Available in three thickness options to suit different climates and conditions. The LD250 version supports temperatures from -12°C to 7°C (10.4°F to 44.6°F). Two bags can be zipped vertically together to create a spacious queen-size sleeping area. Comfortable & Breathable – Made with skin-friendly peach skin velvet lining that resists pilling and cotton leakage. The waterproof fabric keeps moisture out without causing stuffiness. Machine washable with no deformation or loss of softness. Portable & Lightweight – Weighing just 3.5 lbs and compressible into a compact storage size of approximately 10 × 15 inches, each sleeping bag includes a compression bag with straps for easy carrying and storage.
