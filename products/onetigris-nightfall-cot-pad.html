Comparison Notes

3-Way Valve System for Easy Setup & Pack Down Why Choose NIGHTFALL Cot Pad? The OneTigris NIGHTFALL Self-Inflating Cot Mattress is specifically engineered to enhance the comfort, thermal insulation, and support of various outdoor sleeping systems. Whether paired with a folding camping cot or laid directly upon the ground, this self-inflating sleeping pad provides reliable cushioning and support for car camping, off-road expeditions, hunting trips, and basecamp setups. The NIGHTFALL mattress features a built-in 3cm-thick foam core with an R-Value of 4.1, effectively minimizing heat loss to ensure you enjoy comfortable, supportive rest throughout the night. Its unique self-inflating design allows the mattress to automatically expand once the air valve is opened; for a more personalized firmness level, you can also add a small amount of air manually by mouth. Tailored specifically for use with cots, this mattress features integrated elastic straps that securely fasten it to most folding camping beds, while the anti-slip TPU-coated fabric on the underside minimizes shifting during sleep. The sleeping surface is crafted from soft, 30D stretch fabric—offering a comfortable, quiet touch—and combines with a compact roll-up design to make storage and transport effortless and convenient.
