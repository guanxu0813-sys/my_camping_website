Comparison Notes

Why Choose OneTigris Northgaze TC Chimney Tent A Northgaze TC Chimney Tent designed for 2-4 campers, featuring a classic tipi/pyramid shape that’s quick and easy to set up, not to mention aesthetically pleasing for both the camera and the eye. The Northgaze has snow skirts (entrance fabric does not have snow skirts) and a stove jack opening for winter camping, with a porch setup option for you to enjoy your own outdoor patio with a nice cup of coffee. Cooking tools and other small objects can be attached to the MOLLE webbing along the entrance fabric, and you have yourself a perfect home away from home. About terylene cotton: The T/C fabric is known for its rugged quality that’s thicker, heavier and more durable than the usual outdoor gear fabric commonly used. It’s also fire-retardant and safer to use next to campfires, efficiently blocks out sunlight and heat, and its unique material technology enables it to be more water-resistant with each use through the fabric’s interaction with moisture in the outdoors. Fabric performance can be enhanced by setting it up next to a river, stream, or even drizzle.
