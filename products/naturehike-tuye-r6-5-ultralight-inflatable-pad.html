The TuYe™ R6.5 Ultralight Inflatable Sleeping Pad with Reinforced Sidewalls is designed for cold-weather backpacking and alpine adventures. With advanced thermal insulation, reinforced edge support, and a lightweight packable design, it delivers reliable warmth, stability, and comfort for demanding outdoor conditions. Key Features High R-Value Insulation - Featuring an R-value of 6.5 , the multi-layer thermal reflective insulation helps reduce heat loss to the ground, providing dependable warmth for cold-weather camping and high-altitude trekking. Reinforced Sidewall Support - The reinforced sidewall structure enhances edge stability and helps prevent rolling off the pad, offering a more secure and comfortable sleep on uneven terrain. Ultralight & Backpack-Friendly - Designed for hikers and backpackers, the pad combines excellent warmth-to-weight performance with a compact packed size for easy transport on the trail.
