Comparison Notes

Why Choose OneTigris SOLO HOMESTEAD CAMPING TENT 2.0? The upgraded 2.0 version features a non-removable groundsheet providing protection against mosquitoes and rain. Additionally, the functional zipper vestibule offers a versatile setup with storage space for equipment. This rugged shelter, designed for one person, features the distinctive twin-peaked roof and porch mojo, perfect for pairing with a camping chair to immerse in the wilderness. Its unique structure ensures heightened wind-resistance and stability during setup. The T/C version includes a stove jack opening for stove pipes and a detachable tent bottom. Ideal for road trips and motorbike adventures, the SOLO HOMESTEAD is both visually appealing and serves as your home away from home. About terylene cotton: The T/C fabric is known for its rugged quality that’s thicker, heavier and more durable than the usual outdoor gear fabric commonly used. It’s also fire-retardant and safer to use next to campfires, efficiently blocks out sunlight and heat, and its unique material technology enables it to be more water-resistant with each use through the fabric’s interaction with moisture in the outdoors. Fabric performance can be enhanced by setting it up next to a river, stream, or even drizzle.
