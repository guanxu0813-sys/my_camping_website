Comparison Notes

Mongar™ UL 2-Person Ultralight Backpacking Tent is a professional-grade ultralight backpacking tent designed for solo and two-person camping. Minimum weight approx. 1.66 kg, packed weight approx. 1.9 kg, it offers reliable weather protection with 15D nylon fly (PU2000mm+ waterproof silicone coating) and 20D nylon floor (PU3000mm+ waterproof silicone coating). The innovative expandable awning adds extra shelter, while the included footprint and 8.5mm aluminum pole system ensure stability and durability in changing terrain.
