OneTigris Tarp Connection Cap 1. Tarp Connecting Adapter: This accessory allows you to seamlessly connect a tarp to a one-pole tent, providing enhanced stability and resistance to shifting. It ensures a tight and secure connection, offering better shade and protection from rain at the tent entrance. 2. Curved Design & Easy to Use: Featuring an original curved design, this adapter boasts superior strength and durability.
