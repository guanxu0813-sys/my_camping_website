Comparison Notes

Your very own doublewide backcountry bed! Our King Solomon is part of our Doublewide Series, eliminating the need to zip two bags together so there’s nothing keeping you apart from your cuddle bug at camp. Lightweight and packable 650 Downtek™ PFC-Free water-repellent down insulation creates a sleeping bag for two at less weight and bulk than packing two separate bags. Our tried and true Pad Cinch System makes connecting two pads or securing a doublewide pad easier and faster than ever. Now with a full zip off option, you can use the top quilted layer like a comforter or zip together to create a single bag when camping solo. Whether you need room for two, or just prefer sleeping in lightweight, packable doublewide luxury, the King Solomon 20° is the most versatile sleep system out there.
