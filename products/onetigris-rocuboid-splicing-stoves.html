Comparison Notes

Why Choose OneTigris ROCUBOID Splicing Stove? The ROCBOID is a highly versatile piece of kit from ROC for bushcrafters and fellow outdoorsmen to get dirty with, available in 3 versions. The first version features premium quality #304 stainless steel, with its successors the EVIL EYES versions available in both stainless steel and titanium. The ROCUBOID can be set up in multiple ways depending on your creativity and preference, and dismantled for easy carry like a folded treasure map. Note that the ROCUBOID is different from ones seen on the market that are small, cheap, and rust easily; the titanium EVIL EYES version can be washed after use, and each has a nylon case to keep it protected when not in use. Use the ROCUBOID as a wood, alcohol or fuel tablet stove, windshield, Finnish/Swedish torch, or pair it up with a hanging camping pot turned to an outdoor oven. It’s a simple yet solid design that only takes a minute to set up. Get the ROCUBOID and get creative.
