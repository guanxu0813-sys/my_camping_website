Giling UL™ 4-Season 2-Person Backpacking Tent offers spacious comfort in a lightweight, trail-ready design. Built for hikers who prioritize efficiency, its integrated quick-pitch system simplifies setup and reduces overall pack weight for multi-day adventures. Made with 15D silicone-coated nylon rated to PU2000mm+, it balances dependable weather protection with ultralight performance. Key Features True Four-Season Protection - Engineered for year-round backpacking with dependable performance in cold, windy, and variable weather. Integrated Quick-Pitch System - All-in-one inner and fly connection allows faster setup and teardown compared to traditional double-wall tents. Reinforced X-Frame Pole Sleeve Structure - Restriction sleeve system locks poles firmly in place, minimizing movement and delivering exceptional wind resistance and high compression strength. Enhanced Stability in Harsh Conditions - Designed to perform reliably on exposed terrain, maintaining structure under strong wind and pressure. Upgraded Adjustable Ventilation System - Strategically placed vents at the top, vestibule, and base promote airflow and significantly reduce condensation without sacrificing weather protection. Semi-Solid Inner Canopy - Improves warmth retention and blocks cold drafts, making it ideal for winter camping and challenging environments.
