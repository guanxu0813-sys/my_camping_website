Comparison Notes

Constructed with advanced materials for increased performance, the Land Breeze Pro. 1 is part of the enhanced Land Breeze dome tent series. Designed to comfortably accommodate two people, the Land Breeze Pro. 1 is wind-resistant and able to withstand year-round weather. The tent has a side vestibule entry, wall pockets for storage and color-coordinated poles for a simplified set-up. The Land Breeze series features enhanced room temperature control due to mesh skylights with zippered closures, advanced panels and mud skirt design that encourage air flow. Pair the Land Breeze Pro. 1 with the Land Breeze Pro. 1 Ground Sheet and Land Breeze Pro. 1 Inner Mat for additional protection.

At about 6.5 kg, the Snow Peak Land Breeze Pro. 1 sits on the heavier side of our 301-model tents database (median 2.38 kg). Its reference price of 809.95 USD is above the category median of 227.46 USD.
