Comparison Notes

The CW280 is a lightweight down sleeping bag designed for campers, hikers, and backpackers who value warmth without extra bulk. Filled with 280g of 650FP white duck down, it provides reliable comfort in temperatures from -8°C to 11°C (17.6–51.8°F), while weighing only 570g. Ideal for multi-season adventures, it combines portability with superior warmth retention. Key Features: Soft & Skin-Friendly — 20D nylon fabric with a smooth touch, comfortable against the skin Warmth Rated -1°C to 11°C — 280g 650FP white duck down ensures reliable insulation across 3-4 seasons Even Down Distribution — Precision-stitched baffles prevent clumping and maintain consistent warmth Versatile Design — Two-way zip allows use as a quilt or combine two bags into a double sleeping bag Lightweight & Packable — Weighs only 570g, compresses smaller than a 570ml water bottle for easy portability Heat Retention Adjustments — Elastic drawcord and Velcro closure minimize heat loss in colder conditions
