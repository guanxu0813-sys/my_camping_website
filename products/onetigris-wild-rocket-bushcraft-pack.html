Comparison Notes

Why Choose OneTigris WILD ROCKET Bushcraft Pack? What’s essential to survival in the wild? Your outdoor aptitude and the gear on your back. The WILD ROCKET 45L Pack is designed with bushcrafters in mind, featuring US licensed Cordura® nylon for rugged usability, with a 36L capacity main compartment and side/front compartments to carry up to 2 days’ necessities. It’s a straightforward piece of kit for loading up outdoor gear, with tactical durability and versatility in mind. The roll top design allows quick access to your gear, several rows of webbing enable modular attachment of MOLLE add-ons, and on both sides you’ll find a specially designated axe storage space. At the bottom of the pack are straps for rolled up mats or quilts, so that you can utilize all possible space with efficiency. The shoulder straps feature adjustable straps to customize the fit so that the pack hugs the torso and carries weight in the most efficient way. Ideal for 31.5”-47” waist sizes. OneTigris WILD ROCKET Bushcraft Pack ● US licensed Cordura®nylon for extra durability and dirt/water/abrasion resistance, with DWR (durable water repelling) coating at the front and double PU coating at the back, UTX-Duraflex® quick-release buckles, and quality YKK® snag-free zippers ● Roll top design with a 36L main compartment, two 3L buckled side compartments, two 1.5L front zippered pockets, and specially designed axe holders on both sides ● 3 rows of webbing on either side of the pack as well as 2 rows on waist
