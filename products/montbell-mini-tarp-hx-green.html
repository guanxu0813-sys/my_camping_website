Comparison Notes

Our Mini Tarp HX is an excellent lightweight shelter, ideal for gram-conscious thru-hikers and long-distance bikepackers. It’s made from a flame-resistant, 75-denier polyester taffeta that’s been coated with waterproof polyurethane, with up to 1,500 millimetres of water protection. There are six lashing holes, allowing you to pitch the tarp as you please and is compatible with our Aluminium Tarp Poles. Reflective guylines, aluminium pegs and a stuff sack all come included.

At about 0.87 kg, the Montbell Mini Tarp HX (Green) is lighter than 82% of the 58 tarps in our database (category median 4.03 kg). Its reference price of 100 GBP is below the category median of 214.5 GBP.
