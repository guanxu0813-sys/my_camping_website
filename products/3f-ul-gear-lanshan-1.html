Comparison Notes

3F UL Gear Lanshan 1 is a double-wall, single-person trekking-pole tent aimed at ultralight backpacking. It pitches with one trekking pole (about 120–125 cm), offers one door and one vestibule, and uses taped seams. Trail weight is about 920 g. 3-season mesh and 4-season solid-inner options are available.
