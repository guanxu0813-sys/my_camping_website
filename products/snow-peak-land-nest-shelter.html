The Land Nest Shelter is a beginner-level shelter with living and sleeping spaces, providing enough space for a family of four. Based on the Land Nest Dome, the Land Nest Shelter has a symmetrical design and simple frame structure, making it easy to set up for campers of all experience levels. The inner tent can be removed to create a more spacious living area. The shelter material is designed with sun-blocking and temperature-regulating features, making it ideal for camping in any season.
