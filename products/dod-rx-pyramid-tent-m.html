Comparison Notes

The RX Pyramid Tent epitomizes ease and efficiency in outdoor living. Its pyramid-shaped, symmetrical design simplifies setup, ideal for campers at any experience level. This tent is extra tall for comfort and features wide entrances and a skirted outer shell. Can be set up with the inner tent (included) or without the inner tent when you need a floorless space. Easy-to-set-up pyramid shape with a balanced structure. Expansive interior with large side ventilators. Adaptable for all seasons with mesh option and skirted outer shell.
