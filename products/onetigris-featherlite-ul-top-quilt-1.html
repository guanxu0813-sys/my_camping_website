Comparison Notes

Why Choose OneTigris FEATHERLITE UL Top Quilt? If you’ve tried our under-quilts before, you’ll know what kind of gear we’re offering. Different from traditional sleeping bags, this top quit forms a diamond shaped gap for use with waterproof mats or air mattresses. Why? Weight reduction. A lighter load of a sleep system that offers the same protection from the cold, and a much better down distribution so that feathers don’t clog up at the back ending up as deadweight. It weighs a mere 35oz and functions better than a blanket with its “sock bottom” that wraps around your feet to keep you warm. Use this quilt either spread out or sealed at the bottom via hook-and-loop clasps, and enjoy a toasty night in the outdoors. OneTigris FEATHERLITE UL Top Quilt l 20D Ripstop Nylon outer shell with 380T polyester pongee lining, and SEE® polyester filling for an ultra lightweight top quilt l A 3-season quilt suitable for 41°F-59°F (5°C -15°C) temperatures l Weighs only 35oz (1kg), folds up small, and spreads out 6.5ft by 2.8ft offering plenty of sleeping space l Hollowed back design for use with sleep mats for a perfect balance between down distribution and heat retention l Hook-and-loop sealed footbox that can also be unclasped for use as top-quilt l Also recommended for use in hammocks or wrapped outside a sleeping bag for extra warmth Note 1.
