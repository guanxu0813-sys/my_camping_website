Comparison Notes

Intended for laid-back camping trips, our Chronos Dome 4 is an expansive and accommodating four person tent. We’ve maximised living space with our patented Vertical Cross system. The poles cross and point outward at 90 degrees at the apex, vastly increasing overhead space. This, coupled with the spacious vestibule to store mucky gear and the like, gives you plenty of space to rest and chill. The rainfly comes equipped with two mesh panels for excellent breathability, and the seam sealed corners are extremely effective against foul weather.

At about 4 kg, the Montbell Chronos Dome 4 (Thyme) sits on the heavier side of our 301-model tents database (median 2.38 kg). Its reference price of 340 GBP is above the category median of 227.46 GBP.
